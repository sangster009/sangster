# Design Ideation

**Page:** `design-ideations.html`  
**Title:** Design Ideation — Attribution Reporting

---

## Script

- **Opening:** "Here are the **design ideations** we went through. Use the tabs to switch between them."

- **Ideation 1:** "We preserved core product logic while modernizing the UI and emphasizing key KPIs. It's an evolution of the existing dashboard, not a full reimagining yet."

- **Ideation 2:** "We embedded an **in-product agent** to help users interpret and act on their data. That's the first step toward AI-assisted insights."

- **Ideation 3:** "We introduced an **AI-first design** with direct, conversational interaction. The assistant moves to center stage; the dashboard supports it."

- **Transition:** "Next is **Design details**—the UX contract, Why AI-first, information architecture, design system, page anatomy, and micro-interactions—then **Workflow** (current vs future) and **Final design**."

---

## Talking points

1. Ideation 1: modernized UI, key KPIs.
2. Ideation 2: in-product agent for interpretation and action.
3. Ideation 3: AI-first, conversational.
