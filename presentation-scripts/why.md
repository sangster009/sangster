## Script

- **Opening:** "This ties the work to **product strategy and business goals**. Four outcomes we were driving toward."

- **List:** "**Increase** product adoption—make the product stickier by making insights accessible. **Reduce** sales dependency—let users self-serve so sales isn’t the bottleneck. **Shorten** insight turnaround—from days to minutes. **Improve** customer confidence in optimization—so they know what changed and what to do next."

- **Why it matters:** "So the design wasn’t just a UX refresh; it was aligned with adoption, efficiency, speed, and confidence. That’s how we justified the AI-first bet and the design iterations that followed."

- **Transition:** "Next we’ll look at **Design iterations**—how we moved from the current UI through an in-product agent to the AI-first concept that won—then **Design details** and the **UX contract**."
