# Workflow — Current vs Future

**Page:** `workflow.html`  
**Title:** Workflow — Current vs Future

---

## Script

- **Opening:** "This page compares the **current** campaign reporting workflow with the **future** workflow we designed. You can expand steps and use 'Compare with old flow' to see them side by side."

- **Current workflow:** "Same story as before: campaign needs review → login → dashboard → 40+ metrics, no summary → manual drilldowns → export → copy to slides → frustration → contact sales → custom report. Pain points are called out on the steps."

- **Future workflow:** "In the new flow: campaign needs review → login → **AI-generated instant campaign summary** — so they see a summary first, not a wall of metrics. Then they can ask questions, get recommendations, and generate a report in-product. No need to copy into slides or wait on sales for a custom report. The flow is shorter and centered on the AI assistant."

- **Compare mode:** "Use **Compare with old flow** to put current and future side by side. It makes the reduction in steps and the shift from manual to AI-led very clear."

- **Transition:** "Next is **Touchpoints**—a before/after and system improvements—then **Final design** with the hero video and design details, and we’ll close with **Thanks**."

---

## Talking points

1. Current: long path, many pains, sales dependency.
2. Future: AI summary first, questions and recommendations, self-serve report.
3. Compare view highlights the delta.
4. Sets up touchpoints and final design.
