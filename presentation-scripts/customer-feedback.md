# Customer Feedback

**Page:** `customer-feedback.html`  
**Title:** Customer Feedback

---

## Script

- **Opening:** "This page is **Customer Feedback**—voices from our customers on the product. We grouped feedback into categories; you can expand each to read the quotes."

- **Reporting (6 responses):** "Customers asked for the ability to create custom reports on their own, map IDs on their own, and better client-facing guides. They wanted clearer links into custom and partner summary reports, more control in the UI instead of 'behind the curtain,' the ability to create their own custom reports and customize the UI to the KPIs they care about. And a recurring theme: **it takes too long to receive my custom report**."

- **Setup (4 responses):** "On setup: streamline the process with fewer Foursquare members involved, better explanations of the UI or minimums so they can self-serve, and fewer fields and steps to reduce errors and miscommunication."

- **Why it matters:** "This validated what we saw in the workflow: dependency on sales, slow turnaround, and a need for self-serve and prioritization. That fed directly into the problem statement and the AI-first direction."

- **Transition:** "Next is the **Expert UX Review**—a heuristic evaluation of the product—then we’ll structure the **problem** and **why** we built the solution."

---

## Talking points

1. Reporting: self-serve custom reports, clarity, control, faster turnaround.
2. Setup: fewer steps, fewer people, better explanations for self-service.
3. Validates workflow pains and sets up problem + solution.
