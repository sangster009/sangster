## Script

- **Opening:** "This page summarizes **what changed**—before and after, plus system-level improvements. You can use **Show After** to reveal the 'After' column in the table."

- **Before and after (table):** "**Before:** dashboard-first, raw metrics, manual analysis, sales dependency, hours to days. **After:** AI-first, prioritized insights, automated interpretation, self-serve, minutes. That’s the shift in a nutshell."

- **System improvements:** "We closed three gaps. **Interpretation gap**—AI summarizes and highlights what changed. **Decision gap**—AI recommends what to do next. **Operational gap**—users generate reports without sales involvement. So we’re not just reskinning; we’re changing how insights are produced and consumed."

- **Bonus:** "There’s a **Bonus: Admin portal prototype** link if you want to see the admin side; otherwise we go to **Thank you** and wrap up."

- **Transition:** "Next is **Final design**—the hero video and design details—then **Thanks** and back to projects."

## Talking points

1. Before → After: dashboard-first → AI-first, raw → prioritized, manual → automated, sales → self-serve, days → minutes.
2. Interpretation, decision, and operational gaps closed.
3. Optional: admin portal prototype.
4. Leads into final design and close.
