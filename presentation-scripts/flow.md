## Script

- **Opening:** "This is the high-level Ad Tech flow—stakeholder steps from campaign setup to measurement. You can expand each lane to see the details."

- **Lanes (expand as needed):** "**Advertiser / Agency** defines objectives, approves the media plan, funds the campaign. **Media Planner** creates the plan—placements, targeting, budget, creatives, KPIs—and uploads it to the Ad Server. **Ad Server / DSP** trafficks the campaign, delivers ads, reconciles delivery. **Publisher** (e.g. Roku, YouTube, Spotify) serves the ads. **Measurement Platform**—that’s Foursquare—measures attribution and feeds back into reporting."

- **Why it matters:** "Reporting sits downstream of this. Users see results after campaigns run; our job is to make that result set interpretable and actionable instead of a wall of metrics."

- **Transition:** "Next I’ll show the **current workflow**—the campaign reporting experience step-by-step, with the pain points that led to the redesign."

---

## Talking points

1. End-to-end flow: Advertiser → Media Planner → Ad Server → Publisher → Measurement.
2. Reporting is downstream; we make results interpretable and actionable.
3. Sets up current workflow and its pains.
