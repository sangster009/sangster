## Script

- **Opening:** "This is the **Expert UX Review**—a heuristic evaluation of the product. The image summarizes where the experience succeeded and where it fell short against usability principles."

- **If showing the image:** "You can click the image to open it full-size. The evaluation highlights visibility of system status, match between system and real world, user control, consistency, error prevention, recognition over recall, flexibility, and so on—and where the current reporting experience had gaps."

- **Why it’s here:** "It gave us a structured, expert-backed view of the same pains we heard from users and saw in the workflow: too much data, not enough guidance, and lack of clear next steps."

- **Transition:** "Next we’ll do a **Problem deep dive**—operational inefficiency, cognitive overload, and the insight gap—then **Why we built this** and the design iterations that led to the final solution."

## Talking points

1. Heuristic evaluation of the reporting product.
2. Expert view aligns with user feedback and workflow pains.
3. Bridges feedback → structured problem → solution.
