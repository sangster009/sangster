Overview

Who it's for

Agencies and advertisers who need to interpret campaign performance and share insights with clients.

Core problems

Too many metrics, no prioritization, manual report creation, and dependency on sales for custom insights.

Business impact

Slow turnaround, cognitive overload, and lost confidence when data isn't translated into clear next steps.

Project Snapshot

  • Role: Principal Product Designer / PM
  • Ownership: End-to-end experience across reporting lifecycle
  • Team: Eng, Data Science, Sales, Customer Success, Sales Ops
  • Timeline: 1.5 quarters to phase I
